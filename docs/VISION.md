## Problem Statement
Access to timely and organized healthcare remains a challenge due to limited doctor availability, fragmented medical records, and inefficient communication between patients and healthcare providers.

TeleSana addresses this by acting as a **digital mediator between patients and doctors**, enabling easier access to healthcare services through a centralized and user-friendly platform.

---

## Target Users
TeleSana is designed for:

- **Patients**, who need quick access to medical guidance, prescriptions, and their health history in one place.
- **Doctors**, who benefit from streamlined workflows, improved patient record management, and reduced administrative overhead.

The platform aims to ease the workload of doctors while improving accessibility and continuity of care for patients.

---

## What TeleSana Does
- Facilitates interaction between patients and doctors
- Maintains a **centralized digital health passbook** within the patient profile
- Supports medical prescriptions and follow-up tracking
- Reduces dependency on physical visits for initial consultations

---

## Improvements in This Version
In this iteration of TeleSana, the focus is on enhancing usability, accessibility, and intelligence of the system by introducing:

- **Doctor Profiles**  
  Detailed profiles including specialization, availability, and experience.

- **Hospital Discovery with GPS Integration**  
  Location-based hospital and clinic discovery to help patients find nearby medical facilities quickly.

- **Prescription OCR Scanner**  
  Optical Character Recognition (OCR) to scan and digitize medical prescriptions, reducing manual data entry and improving record accuracy.

---

## Vision Going Forward
TeleSana aims to evolve into a smart healthcare assistant that not only connects patients and doctors, but also helps users make informed healthcare decisions efficiently and securely.
